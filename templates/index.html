Built on UNOS kidney offer data (April 2021): 344,798 primary offers, 1,266 acceptances. Acceptance modeled by logistic regression (MLE); future kidney quality drawn from a Beta distribution fit to 1,019 donors; survival and offer-arrival modeled as exponential processes using SRTR-published rates. Decisions compared by Monte Carlo over 8,000 futures, Bayesian posterior over which choice wins, and Thompson sampling across the two actions.
